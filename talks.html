Moral Underpinnings of Attitudes Towards Abortion

February 23, 2025 SPSP Annual Convention, Denver

This poster present our research that examines the moral foundations underlying abortion attitudes by analyzing 11 million tweets. Using NLP models, we identified how moral language shifts depending on whether groups describe themselves or their outgroups, offering insights for communication and policymaking. This poster won the Best Graduate Poster Award!
